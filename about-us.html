Our Vision

Vision Foundation as a charitable trust seeks to make education accessible for every student who dreams to come up in life with a bright career. We do our best for the students to pursue his/her education without any societal pressures.

Vision Foundation also provides aid at the time of natural calamities and disasters. We lend our hands to old-age homes and orphanages whenever possible.

MR.R.SIVASANKARAN , Author of the Trust, laid a foundation for this charitable organization which has developed into what now proudly stands as VISION FOUNDATION. Having been brought-up and educated with many financial difficulties himself, he was more inclined to socially give back to the society especially students facing similar problems in pursuing their education. From 2008, he has been tirelessly working to start a charitable organization to help deprived and deserving students with quality education.

MR.K.SHANMUGAM, Founder of the Trust, was the main backbone for this organization to take shape. A passionate teacher for nearly 34 years, he has immense respect and love for education. And even after retirement he continues his journey for educating students.

MRS.VIMALA SIVASANKARAN, Founder of the trust, apart from taking care of her children and household, she very efficiently manages the functioning of Vision foundation. One of the reasons of the growth of this charity within a short span is due to her immense and self-less support towards the organisation and her family. She has been effortlessly handling all the operations and been there at the grass-root levels providing all the necessary support.
