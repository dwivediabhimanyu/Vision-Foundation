Educational Assistance

Educational Assistance are given to students after a complete background check of their family conditions and performance. We assist the educational needs for students in schools and colleges.

The students who are given financial support are continuously monitored for their improvements in exams and necessary advise are given by Founder of the trust whenever its necessary. The students are also asked to keep in touch with the foundation committee members for their updations. The total amount spent for educational purpose from the year 2008 - 2018 is Rs 61,13,553.

Other Assistance

Aid to school children

Basic necessities like bags, notebooks, etc., are provided by us to schools seeking aid.

Career Guidance

In the current scenario of immense competition and dull job opportunities, we provide career development programs and help our students find jobs suitable for their qualification.

About the Vision Foundation

Vision Foundation as a charitable trust seeks to make education accessible for every student who dreams to come up in life with a bright career. We do our best for the students to pursue his/her education without any societal pressures.
